## Decisions Made

- **Accepted 13 probes as PASS vs planned 15.** All 6 explicit Pass Criteria check boxes tick at 13, full regression-category coverage was achieved, and 0/13 failures was compelling. Documented in device-verification.md Observations as informational shortfall, not a gap.
- **Did not block Phase 34.1 close on missing `path=coldStartBridge` coverage.** The classifier is path-agnostic — `warmDarwin` and `coldStartBridge` run identical logic. Cold-start reliability is a separate OPEN issue independent of insertion-detection simplification. Logged as follow-up coverage gap for production monitoring.
- **Inferred classifier outcomes by rule-replay rather than instrumenting the probe.** The Plan 34-03 privacy contract keeps probe payloads to counts/bools/labels only (no outcome label to avoid creating a probe → outcome → input triangulation risk). Since the classifier is pure (no hidden state), replaying the 7 rules against each probe's (delta, beforeCount, hasTextBefore, hasTextAfter) tuple produces the outcome deterministically. Sufficient evidence for STAB-01 verification without widening the probe surface.
- **Left host-app + scenario columns blank.** The exported log does not carry host-bundle-id per-probe (privacy decision from an earlier phase). User visually confirmed host-app distribution during the session. Classifier-outcome evidence does not depend on host label.
