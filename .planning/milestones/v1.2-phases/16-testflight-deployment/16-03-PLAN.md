<task type="checkpoint:human-action" gate="blocking">
  <name>Task 1: Archive, upload to App Store Connect, and create TestFlight build</name>
  <action>
    Pierre performs these steps in Xcode and App Store Connect. Claude cannot do this (requires Xcode GUI + developer account authentication).
  </action>
  <instructions>
    **Step 1: Verify signing in Xcode**
    1. Open Dictus.xcodeproj in Xcode
    2. Select DictusApp target > Signing & Capabilities
    3. Verify: Team shows your new developer account (not "Personal Team")
    4. Verify: App Group `group.com.pivi.dictus` shows a checkmark (not a warning). If warning, click "Fix Issue"
    5. Repeat for DictusKeyboard target
    6. Build (Cmd+B) to verify no signing errors

    **Step 2: Create app listing in App Store Connect**
    1. Go to https://appstoreconnect.apple.com > My Apps > "+" > New App
    2. Platform: iOS
    3. Name: "Dictus" (fallback: "Dictus - Voice Keyboard" if taken)
    4. Primary language: French
    5. Bundle ID: com.pivi.dictus
    6. SKU: dictus-ios
    7. Privacy Policy URL: https://github.com/Pivii/dictus/blob/main/PRIVACY.md (or your website URL if ready)

    **Step 3: Archive and upload**
    1. In Xcode: set scheme to DictusApp, destination to "Any iOS Device"
    2. Product > Archive (this takes 2-5 minutes)
    3. When archive completes, Organizer opens
    4. Click "Distribute App" > "App Store Connect" > "Upload"
    5. Keep all default options (include bitcode if prompted, include symbols)
    6. Click Upload and wait for processing (5-15 minutes)
    7. Check for email from Apple -- if "Invalid Binary", share the error details

    **Step 4: Submit for Beta App Review**
    1. In App Store Connect > TestFlight, wait for build to show "Ready to Submit"
    2. Fill in "What to Test" field:
       - "Dictus is an offline voice dictation keyboard. To test: 1) Complete onboarding 2) Download a model 3) Open any text field 4) Switch to Dictus keyboard 5) Tap mic and speak"
    3. Add Beta App Review Information:
       - Contact: your email
       - Notes: paste the review notes from REVIEW-CHECKLIST.md (or the review notes from 16-RESEARCH.md)
    4. Submit for Beta App Review

    **Step 5: Create public TestFlight link**
    1. In App Store Connect > TestFlight > Public Link section
    2. Enable public link
    3. Set tester limit (e.g., 10,000 -- maximum)
    4. Copy the public TestFlight link (format: https://testflight.apple.com/join/XXXXXX)

    When done, share:
    - Whether archive and upload succeeded
    - The public TestFlight link
    - Any errors or warnings encountered
  </instructions>
  <resume-signal>Share the TestFlight public link (https://testflight.apple.com/join/...) or describe any errors</resume-signal>
</task>
